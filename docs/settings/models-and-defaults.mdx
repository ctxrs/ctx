## Overview

Model choices are practical trade-offs across quality, speed, and cost.

## What to tune

- Harness
- Model
- Effort level
- Mode

## Default strategy

- Pick one default harness/model combo for most tasks.
- Use higher effort only for hard reasoning or complex refactors.
- Keep routine tasks on lower-latency settings.

## Per-task overrides

Use overrides when a specific task has different needs:

- higher reasoning depth for complex bug hunts
- faster responses for simple repetitive edits
- model swap when one provider is unstable

## Change management

When changing team defaults:

1. test on a few representative tasks
2. compare quality, latency, and cost
3. document why defaults changed
